Model Limitations

  • Forecast trajectories assume broad continuation of historical trends in launches and deployments.
  • Real-world policy shifts, conflict, and rapid technology changes can materially alter future paths.
  • Debris modeling uses a simplified factor-based heuristic rather than a full orbital-physics simulation.

Model Positioning

AstraForecast is designed as a policy-support tool, not a physical orbital simulator.
